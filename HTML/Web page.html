Welcome to Ramoji film city

Ramoji Film City is an integrated film studio complex located in Hyderabad, India. Spread over 1,666 acres,it is the largest integrated film city in the world and as such has been certified by the Guinness World Records as the largest studio complex in the world.It was built by Telugu media tycoon Ramoji Rao in 1996.The Guardian described Ramoji Film City as "city within a city."
